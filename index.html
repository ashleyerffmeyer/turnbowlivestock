About Us

Family owned and operated in Warrensburg, MO

Turnbow Livestock is located on the Turnbow family farm in Warrensburg, Johnson County, Missouri. What started as a hobby for us as a family while working full-time jobs swiftly became a larger passion in our lives.

At Turnbow Livestock, our cattle are part of the family, too. They are born and pasture-raised on our land with no antibiotics, hormones, or steroids.

We offer a variety of beef products currently available for local delivery and purchase at farmers markets. Find us at the Warrensburg and Knob Noster Farmers Markets on Saturdays!

Our farm, animals, and products

Registered and Premium
Black Angus corn-fed Beef

When you buy from Turnbow Livestock, you can be sure our animals are never raised in poor living conditions or full of drugs and hormones. Supporting farmers like Turnbow Livestock allows Johnson County and the Kansas City metro area to have quality agriculture in the local, sustainable food network. This type of humane option with longterm community benefits cannot come from national chains.

From our farm to your table, Turnbow Livestock offers a wide range of items from patties, steaks, briskets, and rotating specials on market days. We also offer the option to purchase a quarter or a half beef! Most importantly, all of our beef is corn-fed, non-antibiotic, and USDA Certified.
